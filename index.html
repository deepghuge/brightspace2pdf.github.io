Privacy

Short, direct, reviewer-friendly.

brightspace2pdf processes only the Brightspace submission attachments you already have access to. It extracts attachment download links from the submission page, fetches those files using your existing Brightspace session, and generates the PDF locally in your browser.

No files are uploaded to external servers by this extension.

Brightspace sometimes loads attachment menus after the page is visible. Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R), scroll a bit so attachments render, then open the extension again.
